How to Master Typing Speed (WPM Guide)

1. Understanding WPM (Words Per Minute)

WPM is the standard metric used to measure typing speed. Our Elite Studio uses the international standard where 1 Word = 5 Characters (including spaces). This ensures that whether you type short or long words, your speed assessment remains fair and accurate.

2. Proper Finger Placement

To reach 80+ WPM, you must use the "Home Row" method. Your fingers should rest on A, S, D, F and J, K, L, ; keys. This muscle memory technique allows you to type without looking at the keyboard.

3. Importance of Accuracy

Speed is useless without accuracy. In a professional environment, errors lead to wasted time. We recommend focusing on 98% accuracy first, then slowly increasing your speed.
